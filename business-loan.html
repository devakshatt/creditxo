Eligibility Criteria: Who Can Apply?
  • Must be an Indian Resident
  • Business Loans are available to sole proprietorship firms engaged in manufacturing, trading, and services with a minimum 2-year business vintage.
  • They should have a strong net worth and a track record of timely repayment.

Documents Required
